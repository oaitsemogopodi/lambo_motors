FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How can I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive by contacting your nearest authorized Lamborghini dealership directly through our dealer locator, or by filling out the contact form on this page and selecting "Test Drive Request" as your subject. Our client advisors will coordinate with your local dealer to arrange your personalized driving experience.

What is the warranty coverage for a new Lamborghini?

All new Lamborghini vehicles come with a 3-year unlimited mileage warranty. Additionally, we offer a 12-year anti-corrosion warranty and 3 years of roadside assistance. For hybrid models, the high-voltage battery is covered for 8 years or 100,000 miles, whichever comes first.

How long does it take to receive a custom-ordered Lamborghini?

The production time for a custom-configured Lamborghini typically ranges between 6-12 months from the time your order is confirmed, depending on the model and current production capacity. Limited edition models may have different timelines. Your dealer will provide you with regular updates throughout the production process.

What financing options are available?

Lamborghini Financial Services offers a range of financing solutions including traditional financing, leasing programs, and balloon payment plans. Our specialists can tailor a financial package to your specific needs. Contact your authorized dealer or our financial services department for personalized rate quotes and terms.

How often does my Lamborghini need servicing?

Lamborghini recommends annual maintenance or every 9,000 miles (15,000 km), whichever comes first. For vehicles driven in extreme conditions or track use, more frequent servicing may be required. All maintenance should be performed by authorized Lamborghini service centers to maintain your warranty coverage.
